Academic Track
Choosing the Academic Track means you have solid plans to proceed to college.
a. Accountancy, Business and Management (ABM) Strand
The ABM Track is for those who plan on taking up Economics, Business Administration, Accountancy and Marketing in college. Those who are planning to take up HRM should choose this too.
b. Humanities and Social Sciences Strand (HUMSS)
This one is for students who are eyeing Writing (particularly, novelists), Political Science, Sociology, Priesthood, Law and Community Studies.
c. Science, Technology, Engineering and Mathematics (STEM) Strand
STEM is the perfect track to choose if you are planning to study Pure and Applied Sciences, Engineering and Mathematics in college. You will be studying Biology, Chemistry, Physics and Calculus in this strand. This is not for the faint-hearted.
d. General Academic Strand
If you’re not yet sure what course to take in college, this strand is the same as you. It teaches Social Science, Humanities, Economics, Management and Disaster Readiness.
